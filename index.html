FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our apps and services.

What is OneBattery app?

OneBattery is an Android battery monitoring app that provides real-time charging speed tracking (ampere & watts), Always-On Display (AOD) features, battery health monitoring, and customizable charge alerts. It's designed for AMOLED screens with battery-saving dark themes.

Is OneBattery free to download?

Yes, OneBattery is free to download from the Google Play Store. The app includes optional in-app purchases for premium features, but core functionality is available at no cost.

What devices support OneBattery?

OneBattery works on all Android devices running Android 7.0 (Nougat) and above. The Always-On Display feature works best on devices with AMOLED or OLED screens for optimal battery efficiency.

How do I contact AppCanopy for support?

You can reach us via email at appcanopy@outlook.com for any support inquiries, feedback, or questions about our apps. We typically respond within 24-48 hours.
